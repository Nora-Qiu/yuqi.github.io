Area 1 — Cache & Delegation
draft-li-dnsop-deep-delegation-scrutiny
Paper: PHOENIX DOMAIN (NDSS 2023) · 9 CVEs
Scrutinize deep delegations and limit cache TTL for deeply nested names to prevent phoenix domain revival attacks.

draft-li-dnsop-enhanced-bailiwick
Paper: MaginotDNS (USENIX Security 2023) · 3 CVEs
Enforce uniform bailiwick checks across all resolver modes, with specific rules for conditional DNS (CDNS) servers.

Area 2 — Query Handling & Amplification
draft-li-dnsop-rd-flag-clarification
Paper: TsuKing (ACM CCS 2023) · 3 CVEs
Clarify that resolvers receiving queries with RD=0 MUST NOT recurse or forward with RD=1.
Datatracker →
draft-li-dnsop-resolver-resilience
Paper: DNSBomb (IEEE S&P 2024) · 10 CVEs
Best Current Practice: response pacing with randomized delays and shorter upstream timeouts (1.5–3s) to prevent pulsing DoS.
Datatracker →
draft-li-dnsop-ecs-aggregation-fix
Paper: RebirthDay (ACM CCS 2025) · 35 CVEs
Updates RFC 7871: add “no-ECS-support” state tracking per zone to prevent birthday-attack cache poisoning via ECS prefix diversity.

Area 3 — Packet Pre-processing
draft-li-dnsop-response-preprocessing
Paper: TUDOOR (IEEE S&P 2024) · 33 CVEs
